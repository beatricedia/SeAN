User guide

Introduction

This is a technical document intended to give an overview on how our application is to be used, and the features it has.

Navigating Allergies WorkFlow

The main page of the app is News feed with the latest added allergies. There are also General and Application statistics which can be downloaded.

If we want to find quickly a specific allergy, we can use the search field by typeing keywords.

Once we accessed an allergy, we will be redirect to the allergy page wher relative informations will show up like: short description, symptoms, prevention methods, treatment and medication. Also a text field for comments and share button.

Here we can find statistics in html and pdf format ready to be downloaded for those who are interested.

We can share this information with our group of friends by pressing the share on facebook button.

We are able to select if we want to share this allergy infos only with a friend or a certain group or with all of our friends on social network.

Down the page is "add a comment" section and after press send button, the comment will appear below.

If you don't have an account, you can easily register.

Then you can log in.

After you are on your account, you can add an allergy.

You can see your saved allergy and other users suggestions of allergies in the suggestions page.

If you are logged in as admin, you can give your approvel to send the suggested allergy tothe News page.

There you also have Profile settings where you can select which type of notifications you'd like to recieve and also what allergies you have.

If you choosed to recieve notification of what's new in the selected categories, it will be shown as a popup after you log in.

If you choosed to recieve notification when somebody will add a comment on your selected allergies, it will be shown as a popup after you log in.

If you choosed to recieve seasonal notification, it will be shown as a popup after you log in.
